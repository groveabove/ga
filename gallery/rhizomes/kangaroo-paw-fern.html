The Rhizome Collection / Kangaroo Paw Fern Chandelier

Dimensions: 12 in. W x 26 in. H* x 15 in. D
*customizable downrods

Materials: Porcelain, raw brass, & remnant acrylic.

Sourcing is one of our highest priorities! Grove Above’s pure white porcelain is mixed in-house from domestic USA materials and a little bit from New Zealand, home of the native Kangaroo Paw Fern.

In addition to our in-house porcelain, this particular piece features an Italian cast brass canopy. Pre-fabricated brass components and electrical components were sourced from a small USA business. In-house fabricated brass components were crafted from USA raw brass. The acrylic was found in a local store’s scrap bin and was originally manufactured by Plastiglas de Mexico S.A. de C.V. Our silicone bushings were custom cast from USA silicone, and even the washers and nuts are USA made. Please reach out if you’d like to know more.
