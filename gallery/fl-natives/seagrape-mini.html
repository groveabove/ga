The Florida Native Collection / Seagrape Mini Chandelier

Dimensions: 13 in. W x 22 in. H x 11 in. D

Materials: Porcelain, platinum silicone, brass, & steel

Seagrapes are a native Florida tree, branching upwards with long spindly stalks and showcasing beautiful saucer-shaped leaves. While they seem a little alien to the unfamiliar, they are ubiqitous throughout the state. Read more from the University of Florida.

These particular leaves were cast from fresh leaves off of a Seagrape in our front yard, originally sourced from a local native nursery. If you look closely, this piece culminates with a silicone bud.
