The Florida Native Collection / Seagrape Chandelier

Dimensions: 20 in. W x 56 in. H x 16 in. D

Materials: Porcelain, brass, & steel

Seagrapes are a native Florida tree, branching upwards with long spindly stalks and showcasing beautiful saucer-shaped leaves. While they seem a little alien to the unfamiliar, they are ubiqitous throughout the state. Read more from the University of Florida.

These particular leaves were cast from a fresh leaf off of a Seagrape in our front yard, originally sourced from a local native nursery. It hasn't fruited yet, but here's hoping!
